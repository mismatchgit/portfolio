Research Work

Published and On Going Research

February 2024 - July 2024

Enhancing Diagnostic Accuracy in Bangla: LLM Approaches to Gender-Based Medical Specialty Prediction

Manuscript submitted to the ICTAI 2024 Conference in USA

This research explores the use of Large Language Models (LLMs) both open source and closed source to enhance diagnostic accuracy in the Bangla language by predicting gender-based medical specialties. By leveraging advanced natural language processing techniques, the study aims to improve healthcare outcomes in Bangladesh, offering a tailored approach to medical diagnosis that considers linguistic and gender-specific nuances.

May 2024 - August 2024

Towards Resilient Agriculture in Bangladesh: An Explainable AI Approach

Manuscript to be submitted in the ICREST 2024 Conference in Bangladesh

This research aims to develop a predictive model for flood occurrence in Bangladesh, with the goal of minimizing crop loss. By integrating explainable AI, the project seeks to provide transparent and actionable insights into flood patterns, helping to enhance preparedness and agricultural resilience in a region highly vulnerable to seasonal flooding.

July 2024 - Present

Seasonal Power Balancing in Bangladesh: A Hybrid Approach with Explainable AI

Working on this Currently

This research focuses on developing predictive models to optimize power consumption in Bangladesh by leveraging a combination of solar, wind, and hydro energy sources. Utilizing seasonal data and integrating explainable AI, the project aims to create a dynamic and efficient energy supply strategy that adapts to the unique monsoon-driven climate patterns in the region.
